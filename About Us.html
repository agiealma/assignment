Vasco Da Gama

He was a portuguese who came to the Kenyan coast with an aim of gaining raw materials and spreading christianity to the Africans.He came with a view to change Africans and also gain from them.He was also able to settle in Mombasa where he created his Fort which he lived in.His ship had a compass which is now a recreational site known as the Vasco Da Gama pillar.Many tourists both local and international visit this beautiful scenery.
